Weather safety

If an incoming thunderstorm is severe, don't go outside, stay indoors and find shelter.

If outside, move to a hard top car and refrain from opening the windows...

Tornado precautions

If a tornado warning is issued find a lowest portion of the sturdy building and avoid windows at all costs...

if your driving, pull over to a safe spot and find shelter away from trees...

Flood precautions

If a flash flood warning is issued, seek to higher ground or evacuate the area...

Do not swim in floodwaters as 6 inches can knock you down and avoid basements at all costs...
